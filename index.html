How to Play

  • Goal: Move all disks from Peg A to Peg C
  • Rule: A larger disk cannot be placed on a smaller one
  • Click a disk to select it, then click a peg to move it
  • Or drag and drop disks between pegs
  • Try to solve with the fewest moves possible!

Hint

The minimum number of moves for 5 disks is 31.

Formula: 2n - 1 moves (where n = number of disks)
